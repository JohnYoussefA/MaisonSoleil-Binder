Lutron Maestro MS-OPS2 Flash Cheat Sheet

MS-OPS2 family occupancy sensor. Hold button(s) until lens flashes, then release on your chosen flash count.

Version 9 Date 2026-01-17

? How programming works

The lens is your feedback. Count flashes like a tiny Morse code for switches.

  • Hold the indicated button(s) until the lens starts flashing (about 5 s).
  • The lens flashes once per option, starting at option 1.
  • Release immediately after the flash number you want. The setting saves on release.
  • AUX button is behind the wallplate (remove wallplate to access AUX).
Test Mode: Auto exits after 5 min, or when any button is pressed.

A Large On/Off button - Timeout (auto off delay)

Hold LARGE, then release after:

Flash count Setting
1 flash Test Mode (short timeout, coverage test)
2 flashes 1 minute
3 flashes 5 minutes
4 flashes 15 minutes
5 flashes 30 minutes

! Quick behavior note

  • In Occupancy modes, a manual OFF can turn back ON after about 25 s if Off while occupied is disabled.
  • In Vacancy mode, lights can still turn ON during a short grace period right after timeout.

B AUX button - Sensor mode

Hold AUX (behind wallplate), then release after:

Flash count Mode
1 flash Occupancy: Auto ON + Auto OFF
Off while occupied DISABLED
2 flashes Vacancy: Manual ON + Auto OFF
3 flashes Auto ON daylight sensing + Auto OFF
Off while occupied ENABLED
4 flashes Occupancy: Auto ON + Auto OFF
Off while occupied ENABLED
Daylight Sensing (Mode 3): If it turns ON when the room is bright, or does not turn ON when dim, press LARGE within 5 s of entering to teach the threshold.

C Large + AUX together - Advanced settings

Hold LARGE + AUX together, then release after:

Flash count Setting
1 flash Motion sensitivity LOW
2 flashes Motion sensitivity HIGH
3 flashes Factory default: Return all settings to default

Tip Count flashes out loud. The switch does not care, but your future self will.

i Button map (quick)

  • LARGE front button controls timeout.
  • AUX behind wallplate controls sensor mode.
  • LARGE + AUX together controls advanced settings.
